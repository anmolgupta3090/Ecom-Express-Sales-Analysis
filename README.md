# Ecom-Express-Sales-Analysis
Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Sales Analytics Dashboard built in Power BI, inspired by the business model of E-commerce logistics companies like Ecom Express.The dashboard provides insights into revenue trends, product performance, regional sales distribution, and customer behavior, enabling data-driven decision-making.

Objective
Analyze sales performance across products, categories, and regions
Identify top-performing and underperforming segments
Track key business KPIs such as revenue, orders, and cancellations
Enable quick filtering using quarterly slicers for time-based analysis

Tools & Technologies Used
Power BI – Data visualization & dashboard creation
Microsoft Excel / CSV – Data storage and preprocessing
DAX – Measures and calculated fields

Dataset Description

The analysis is based on structured data including:

Orders (date, value, status)
Products (category, price, ratings)
Customers (location/state)

Key Metrics
Total Revenue: ₹1.25 Billion
Total Orders: 15.69K
Average Order Value: ₹112.85K
Lost Revenue: ₹525.42 Million
Cancellation Rate: 29.72%

Key Insights
Revenue Concentration: Top product categories (Laptops & Mobiles) contribute a significant share of total revenue, with laptops alone driving the highest category revenue (~₹0.4bn).
Product Performance: High-value products like MacBook Air and OnePlus 9 are among the top revenue generators, indicating strong demand for premium devices.
Regional Dominance: Maharashtra and Gujarat emerge as leading markets, each contributing over ₹0.2bn+ revenue, highlighting geographic concentration.
Seasonal Trend: Revenue remains stable around ₹0.35–0.38bn for the first three quarters but drops sharply to ~₹0.15bn in Q4, indicating potential seasonality or operational issues.
Customer Behavior: A relatively high AOV (₹112K+) suggests fewer but high-value transactions.
Risk Indicator: Nearly 30% cancellation rate leads to ₹525M+ lost revenue, representing a major inefficiency in the order lifecycle.
Product Quality Signal: Products like Bose Speaker and Samsung Galaxy maintain high average ratings (~4+), indicating strong customer satisfaction in select segments.
💼 Business Insights
Reduce Cancellations: Even a 5–10% reduction in cancellation rate could recover a substantial portion of the ₹525M lost revenue.
Focus on High-Value Segments: Premium products are driving revenue—targeted marketing and inventory planning can further boost sales.
Regional Expansion Opportunity: Heavy reliance on a few states suggests scope to improve penetration in underperforming regions.
Quarterly Drop Investigation: The sharp decline in Q4 revenue needs investigation (supply chain, demand shift, or operational constraints).
Leverage High-Rated Products: Promote highly rated products to improve conversion rates and customer trust.
