# Ecom Express Sales Analysis

## Project Overview
This project presents an interactive **Sales Analytics Dashboard** built in **Power BI**, inspired by the business model of E-commerce logistics companies like Ecom Express.  

The dashboard provides insights into **revenue trends, product performance, regional sales distribution, and customer behavior**, enabling data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objective
- Analyze sales performance across products, categories, and regions  
- Identify top-performing and underperforming segments  
- Track key business KPIs such as revenue, orders, and cancellations  
- Enable quick filtering using quarterly slicers for time-based analysis  

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- **Power BI** – Data visualization & dashboard creation  
- **Microsoft Excel / CSV** – Data storage and preprocessing  
- **DAX** – Measures and calculated fields  

---

## Dataset Description
The analysis is based on structured data including:

- **Orders:** date, value, status  
- **Products:** category, price, ratings  
- **Customers:** location/state  

---

## Key Metrics
- **Total Revenue:** ₹1.25 Billion  
- **Total Orders:** 15.69K  
- **Average Order Value:** ₹112.85K  
- **Lost Revenue:** ₹525.42 Million  
- **Cancellation Rate:** 29.72%  

---

## Key Insights
- **Revenue Concentration:** Top product categories (**Laptops & Mobiles**) contribute a significant share of total revenue, with laptops alone generating approximately **₹0.4bn**.  
- **Product Performance:** High-value products like **MacBook Air** and **OnePlus 9** are among the top revenue contributors, indicating strong demand for premium devices.  
- **Regional Dominance:** **Maharashtra** and **Gujarat** lead in revenue generation, each contributing over **₹0.2bn+**, highlighting geographic concentration.  
- **Seasonal Trend:** Revenue remains stable around **₹0.35–0.38bn** for the first three quarters but drops sharply to **~₹0.15bn in Q4**, indicating possible seasonality or operational issues.  
- **Customer Behavior:** A high **Average Order Value (₹112K+)** suggests fewer but high-value transactions.  
- **Risk Indicator:** A **29.72% cancellation rate** results in **₹525M+ lost revenue**, pointing to inefficiencies in the order lifecycle.  
- **Product Quality Signal:** Products like **Bose Speaker** and **Samsung Galaxy** maintain strong average ratings (**4+**), indicating high customer satisfaction.  

---

## Business Insights
- **Reduce Cancellations:** Even a **5–10% reduction** in cancellation rate could recover a significant portion of the **₹525M lost revenue**.  
- **Focus on High-Value Segments:** Premium products are key revenue drivers—targeted marketing and inventory optimization can boost performance further.  
- **Regional Expansion Opportunity:** Heavy reliance on a few states indicates scope to improve penetration in underperforming regions.  
- **Investigate Q4 Decline:** The sharp revenue drop in Q4 requires analysis (supply chain issues, demand shifts, or operational constraints).  
- **Leverage High-Rated Products:** Promoting highly rated products can improve conversion rates and customer trust.
